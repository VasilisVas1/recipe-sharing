Tater Tot Chili Cheese Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chili
  • 2 cups tater tots
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

    • Instructions

      • Heat oven to 35
      • Add chili to cover the bottom of a casserole dish.
      • Top the chili with frozen tater tots. Cook in oven for 20 minutes.
      • Carefully top casserole with shredded cheese and cook for an additional 10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
      • Serve.
      • FREEZER MEAL INSTRUCTIONS
      • Add the chili to a freezer safe container. Top with tater tots and remove as much air as possible before freezing. Leave in the freezer for up to 3 months.
    • To cook
      • Remove from freezer and bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 45 minutes.
      • Remove foil, top with cheese, and then bake for an additional 10 minutes.
      • Serve.
      • CROCKPOT FREEZER MEAL INSTRUCTIONS
      • Add chili to freezer safe bag.
      • Remove air and freeze for up to 3 months.
      • To cook: Thaw chili in refrigerator overnight.
      • Add chili to the crockpot and cook on low for 4 hours. Stir.
      • Top chili with tater tots and cook for an additional 2 hours. Cover with cheese, and cook for an additional hour.
      • Serve.
