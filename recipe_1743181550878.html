Strawberry Cucumber Refresher

Ingredients

  • 1/3 large cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 lemon, sliced
  • 5-6 large mint leaves
  • 2 large strawberries, sliced
  • 2 cups fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 2 1/2 cups water

    • Instructions

      • Add the strawberries, cucumber, lemon, and mint to a pitcher or carafe.
      • Pour in water.Refrigerate the drink for at least 30 minutes so that the water can be properly infused.Enjoy chilled or with some ice cubes!
