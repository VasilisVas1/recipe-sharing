Frozen Fruit Dessert

Ingredients

  • 5 Bananas sliced
  • 1 can Lemonade, frozen - (6 oz)
  • 1 can Orange juice, frozen - (6 oz)
  • 1 can Pineapple chunks - (12 oz)
  • 20 ounces Frozen strawberries

    • Instructions

      • Mix all ingredients and freeze in a large contianer. When ready to serve, thaw until slushy. Good alone or with plain cake.A serving equals 1 cup.
