Banana Chocolate Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 4-5 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter

    • Instructions

      • In food processor puree bananas and avocado.
      • Add in cocoa powder and peanut butter...puree till mixed in.Scoop into bowl or individual serving dishes.Chill and ENJOY!!
