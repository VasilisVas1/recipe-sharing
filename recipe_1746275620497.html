Garden Fresh Heirloom Tomato, Pepper, and Cucumber Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 inches heirloom and/or regular tomatoes (in a variety of colors if possible), cored and cut into 1/2 chunks
  • 1 inch large green pepper, cored, seeds cleaned out, and cut into 1/2 pieces
  • 1/2 inch cucumber, peeled, seeds removed, and cut into 1/2 pieces
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 10 large basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 large garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon red wine or balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • salt and pepper, generously used to taste.

    • Instructions

      • Blend all ingredients in a medium bowl.
      • Add more of any ingredient that you prefer.
      • Salt and pepper to taste.
      • Allow the salad to set for a about 15 minutes for all of the juices to blend well together.
