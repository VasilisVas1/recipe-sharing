Kappa Maki

Ingredients

  • 2 Japanese cucumber, cut into long sticks
  • 4 inches sheets of nori (dried seaweed), cut half
  • 4 cups sushi rice

    • Instructions

      • Put a half-size nori on top of a bamboo mat (makisu).
      • Spread a half cup of sushi rice on top.
      • Place cucumber sticks lengthwise on the rice.
      • Roll up the bamboo mat, pressing forward to shape the sushi into a cylinder. Press the bamboo mat firmly with hands.Unwrap the bamboo mat.
      • Cut the sushi roll into bite-size pieces.
