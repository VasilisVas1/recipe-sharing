Nutella Banana Pancakes German Style

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 120ml carbonated water
  • 4 eggs
  • 400ml milk
  • 4 tablespoons nutella
  • 6 tablespoons oil
  • 400g white flour

    • Instructions

      • Separate the egg whites from the yolks.Beat the egg whites until they are stiff.
      • Mix egg yolks, flour, water, and milk in a big bowl until smooth.Gently fold the stiff egg whites in.
      • Heat a large pan to medium to high heat with 1 tablespoon of oil per pancake. Distribute about 1 ladle of the batter in the pan and fry for about 2 minutes per side.If you don't want to eat-cook-eat-cook, you could pre-heat the oven and make all the pancakes, keeping them warm in the oven until all are prepared before you dig in.
